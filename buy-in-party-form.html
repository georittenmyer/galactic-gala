✦ Host an Experience

Host a Buy-In Party

Buy-in parties are fun, community-hosted mini events that raise money for Salmon Bay. You can host something simple like a game night at your house or offer something unique like a pastry baking class for a small group. Hosts donate the experience, so every ticket sold goes directly to supporting FOSB and our school.

Ready to create something fun and meaningful? Fill out the form below or reach out with any questions at FOSBparties@gmail.com.
