✦ Fundraising Goal

Reach for $120,000

Your support goes directly to Salmon Bay to help fund next year’s school budget. Through FOSB, these funds help bridge the gap left by district funding, support essential staff, and provide resources for scholarships, committees, and the programs and activities that make Salmon Bay such an incredible community.

Don't miss out on the fun! 🐟🎶
