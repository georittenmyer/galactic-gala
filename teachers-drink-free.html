Nourish Our Staff

We're a small team of volunteers who organize this event because we love the school. Last year we hosted it with zero budget—everything was donated or paid for out of pocket by the team as our way of giving back.

This year we're setting up a small "Wine & Dine Our Teachers" fund. It's not an official FOSB fundraiser and it's not tax-deductible—just a friendly pool of funds (capped at $500) to provide snacks and drink tokens for our teachers during the event.

If you'd like to chip in, we'd appreciate it! 🍷✨
