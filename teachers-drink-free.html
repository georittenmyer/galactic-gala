Fuel the Faculty 🚀

After much demand last year, we're excited to introduce the "Fuel the Faculty" fund to celebrate our stars of the evening, our teachers and faculty. This fund (capped at $300) is an informal Venmo pool that provides food and drink tokens for our educators on the night of the event.

This isn't a fundraiser or a formal donation. It's simply a thoughtful way to make the evening run smoothly and spread genuine appreciation to all the teachers and staff who make our community special. Plus, it gives everyone a chance to celebrate their favorite teachers in a way that feels personal and meaningful. Tokens will be handed out to the faculty night of as a final cap to Teacher Appreciation Week!
