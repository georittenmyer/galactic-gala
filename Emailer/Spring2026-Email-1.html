We're two Salmon Bay K‑8 dads helping organize this year's Galactic Gala Spring Auction, our school's biggest community fundraiser.

We're reaching out to local businesses to ask if you might consider donating an item to the auction. Donations can be anything, a gift certificate, product, service, or a unique experience.

Salmon Bay is a Seattle public choice school that draws families from across North Seattle, with a strong community centered in Ballard. The auction helps fund essential staff, classroom support, and programs that make our school so special.

Businesses that donate are recognized on our auction site and shared with hundreds of Salmon Bay families who love supporting the local businesses that support their school.
