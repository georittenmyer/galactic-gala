Privacy Statement

The Friendly Version

The Official-Sounding Part

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you choose to share with us. When you use this website, we may collect basic information such as your name, email address, phone number, bidding history, and payment details for the sole purpose of:

  • Processing donations and auction purchases
  • Communicating important event updates
  • Delivering items, receipts, or thank-you messages
  • Making this fundraiser run smoothly

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise distribute your personal information to third parties for marketing or other unrelated purposes. Your information is used only for this fundraiser and related community activities.

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure. However, as with any website run by sleep-deprived volunteers fueled by coffee and good intentions, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

The Human Part

We are a group of volunteer parents donating our time to fundraise and bring together our awesome community. We are committed to making this experience fun, fair, and positive for everyone.

If something is wrong, confusing, imperfect, or not up to your standards, we sincerely apologize. Please know it is never due to negligence or any intention to disrupt, frustrate, or harm anyone. We are volunteers, and while we will absolutely make mistakes and won't always get everything right, we are all here for the same reason: we care deeply about our school, our community, and our kids.

We genuinely want Salmon Bay to succeed and for every student to thrive.

If you ever have a concern, question, or issue, please reach out to us directly. We promise to listen, treat you with respect, and do our very best to help and accommodate however we can.

The Spirit of It All

By participating in this fundraiser, you are helping build something special for our kids and community. We are grateful for your support, your patience, and your kindness.
