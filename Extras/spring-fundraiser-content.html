The Galactic Gala

Date: Friday, May 8th, 2026 • 7:00 – 10:00 PM
Location: Old Stove Gardens, Seattle, WA
(All purchases, ticket sales and donations go directly toward our $120,000 fundraising goal)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Galactic Gala?
The Galactic Gala is our annual spring fundraiser celebrating our community while supporting Salmon Bay K-8. It's more than just a party—it's an opportunity to connect with fellow parents, educators, and community members, while directly supporting the programs and resources that make our school special.

Who can attend?
This is an adults-only event! It's a perfect night out for parents, guardians, and community members to enjoy good food, music, and company while supporting our school.

What do I wear?
Come as you are, or dress to fit our galactic theme! Costumes and creativity are highly encouraged. Whether you come in casual clothes or full cosmic attire, we celebrate all levels of participation.

What does the fundraiser support?
All proceeds from The Galactic Gala support the FOSB budget for the next school year. Your participation helps us provide:

  • a generous annual grant to Salmon Bay school that helps close the gap in district funding, supporting essential staff
  • scholarships so every student can access programs like SEP (Spring Enrichment Program), overnight camps, field trips, and activities
  • classroom and program supplies across all grade levels
  • support for school events, yearbooks, and community-building activities
  • resources that enhance curriculum and student opportunities beyond what the district provides

Can I participate if I can't attend the event?
Absolutely! You can bid in our online auction, join a Buy-In Party, or make a direct donation through Givebutter.com. Every contribution makes a difference!

What are Buy-In Parties?
Buy-In Parties are fun, community-hosted experiences that raise money for Salmon Bay. Parents and community members host everything from game nights to special classes or activities. When you buy a ticket to a party, your payment goes directly to FOSB and our school.

How can I get involved beyond attending?
We're always looking for volunteers! Whether you want to knock on doors, run a social media campaign, help hang decorations, or contribute in any other way—we'll take it! Fill out our volunteer form or reach out directly to sbk8auction@gmail.com to get involved.
